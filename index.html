Welcome to Wonderland

Begin your journey where curiosity first stirred, and follow Alice as her ordinary afternoon unfolds into something wonderfully impossible.

Mushroom by adege, Pixabay

It began on an ordinary afternoon – sunlight, a quiet riverbank, a book with no pictures, and a girl drifting toward sleep. Nothing strange at all… until a white rabbit rushed by, muttering about being late.

Curiosity pulled Alice after him, down a deep well and into a world where very few things stay the size they started. Doors shrink, hallways stretch, animals argue, and tears can turn into oceans. Wonderland is a place where questions don’t behave and rules change with every step.

If you’re ready to follow Alice through locked doors, growing spells, puzzled creatures, and races with no beginning or end, then step inside. Wonderland is waiting for you.
